Projects We Live For

Lofty Labs consultants have an average 10 years experience in software engineering, with specializations in Python, Django, ElasticSearch, and many more technologies. Our consultants have worked in small businesses, media organizations, and enterprise supporting everything from marketing departments to big data.

Large-scale Web Applications / APIs

Django has proven itself a top tier framework for building high tech and massively scalable applications and APIs, like Pinterest and Instagram. If you need scale, we'll architect it.

Data Analysis and Pipelines

Need to stream big data into your application? We build distributed systems to process huge volumes of data in the background. Are you sitting on a huge pile of data and need custom tools to dissect it? We build software custom fitted to your data that help you get straight to insights.
